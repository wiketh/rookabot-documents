Privacy Policy for Mot Bot
Last updated: 21.06.2026

We operate the bot ("RookaChoo"). We respect your privacy.
## Data We Collect / Use:

- User IDs and Server IDs to use it in bot answers, profile's and leaderboard.
- Message metadata (word count, emoji usage frequency) to generate leaderboard state and game answers.
- We do NOT store the full text of your messages. Once the message is processed for counters, the text is discarded from memory.
## Data Storage & Deletion:

- Data is stored securely in a database.
- You can request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at Telegram @rbspt111.
## Third Parties:

We do not share or sell any data to third parties.
